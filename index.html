⚠ Owner Password Recovery

To reset your password, you must directly edit the Users sheet in your Master Google Sheet:

  1. Open your Master Sheet in Google Drive
  2. Go to the Users tab
  3. Find your email row
  4. Edit the Password column directly
  5. Come back and login with new password

🔒 Why no email reset?

This is a local Google Sheets system — passwords are stored in your own sheet, not on any server. You have full control to change them directly.
